ABOR was represented at both AGU Ocean Sciences, the premiere ocean sciences conference that brings together the international ocean research community, and a smaller but equally important BioCarbon workshop, focused on constraining the ocean's biological carbon pumps. ABOR research into climate dynamics, microplastics, and fundamental biological carbon storage was presented.
